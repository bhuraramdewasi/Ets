Our recent events

We have partnered with Heads up for Tails and Food Orbit Events, two highly respected brands in our community, to promote awareness for community animals. Our shared mission is to spread compassion and advocate for animal welfare. We are honored to work alongside these reputable companies and believe that our partnership will have a positive impact on the lives of animals in our community. This collaboration allows us to expand our reach and amplify our message to a wider audience. Together, we can create a more compassionate world for animals.

About Prakriti Farm Foundation

We are Prakriti Farm Foundation, a non-profit organisation dedicated to making the lives of voiceless souls better by providing food and medical care. Lives of Stray animals are hard and we try to show compassion towards them. Our team is spread across India’s various cities and regions. We are not heroes but just ‚ÄúMango People ” living next door with a willingness to help animal society and mother nature by doing a simple act of kindness. Our volunteers on the ground are responsible for feeding and medically rescuing street animals. In order to develop future generations of responsible and compassionate individuals towards nature and animals, we offer internships to university and college students. Also, we run various programs; details can be found in the What we do Section.
